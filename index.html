Brief Bio

Junwei Cheng is a Ph.D. candidate at South China Normal University. His research interests include community detection and graph data mining. He has published papers in several prestigious journals and conferences, including TKDE, AAAI, SIGIR, CIKM, ESWA, and Information Fusion.
